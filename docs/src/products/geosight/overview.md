# GeoSight Overview

## Why Choose this Product?

GeoSight provides a ready-to-use platform for visualising, analysing, and sharing geospatial data, making it easier for organizations to turn complex datasets into actionable insights. Here’s what makes GeoSight stand out:

- **Powerful Dashboard-Based Analysis**

    Create interactive dashboards that combine maps, indicators, and charts in a single view, enabling users to explore data intuitively without requiring advanced GIS skills.

    <br>

- **Subnational Insight & Risk Analysis**

    Analyse data at multiple administrative levels to identify high-risk or vulnerable areas, supporting targeted interventions and better planning.

    <br>

- **Flexible Data Integration**

    Connect and integrate datasets from spreadsheets, APIs, and external geospatial services, allowing you to build a unified view of your data.

    <br>

- **Real-Time & Dynamic Data Support**

    Incorporate live or frequently updated datasets, such as hazard alerts or event data, to support timely decision-making.

    <br>

- **Advanced Visualisation Tools**

    Use choropleth maps, charts, widgets, and comparison tools to better understand relationships and trends within your data.

    <br>

- **Collaboration & Data Sharing**

    Share dashboards, embed visualisations in external platforms, or distribute datasets in common formats like Excel.

    <br>
